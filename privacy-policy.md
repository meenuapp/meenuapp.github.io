# Privacy Policy for MeenuApp Applications
Last Updated: April 14, 2026

MeenuApp ("we," "our," or "us") operates several mobile applications (the "Service" or "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Services and the choices you have associated with that data.

### 1. Information Collection and Use
We do not require users to create an account or provide personal identification information (such as name or email) to use our Apps. However, we use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

### 2. Information Collected by Third Parties
Our Services integrate APIs and SDKs from third-party providers (like Google) to provide advertising and app functionality. These third parties may collect information such as your device's Advertising ID, IP address, and usage statistics.

* **Google Play Services:** Used for game distribution and platform features. [Privacy Policy](https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/)
* **AdMob (Google Mobile Ads):** We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect data to personalize ads and analyze performance. [Privacy Policy](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543?hl=en)

### 3. Permissions
Our Apps may request the following permissions to function correctly:
* **Internet & Network State:** To load advertisements and sync app content.
* **Vibration:** To provide haptic feedback during gameplay.
* **Notifications:** To send you reminders or daily rewards (you can opt-out at any time in device settings).

### 4. Local Storage
We use local storage (SharedPreferences) to store your progress, settings, and scores locally on your device. This data is not uploaded to our servers.

### 5. Children’s Privacy
Our Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

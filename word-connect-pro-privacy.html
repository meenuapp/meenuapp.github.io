Privacy Policy for Word Connect Pro

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

EmbroBees ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Word Connect Pro mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not require users to create an account or provide personal identification information. However, we use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

2. Information Collected by Third Parties

  • Google Play Services: Used for distribution and platform features. Privacy Policy
  • AdMob (Google Mobile Ads): Used to display advertisements. Privacy Policy

3. Permissions

  • Internet Access: To load ads and sync daily challenges.
  • Vibration: For haptic feedback during gameplay.
  • Notifications: For daily rewards and challenge reminders.

4. Local Storage

We use SharedPreferences to store your progress (levels, coins, piggy bank, stars, and streaks) locally on your device. This data is never uploaded to our servers.

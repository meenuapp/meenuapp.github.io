Privacy Policy for Embro Bees

Last Updated: April 21, 2026

EmbroBees ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Embro Bees mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Services.

1. Information Collection and Use

Embro Bees is a project management tool for embroidery. We do not collect personal information like your name or email. All project data is stored locally.

2. Information Collected by Third Parties

  • Google Play Services: Used for distribution and platform features. Privacy Policy
  • AdMob (Google Mobile Ads): Used to display advertisements. Privacy Policy

3. Permissions

  • Storage Access: Required to save and load embroidery patterns on your device.
  • Internet Access: To load advertisements and community patterns.

4. Data Retention

All your project data, pattern uploads, and progress are stored locally on your device. This data is not synchronized with our servers. Deleting the app will remove this local data.

5. Children’s Privacy

Embro Bees is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at: contact@embrobees.com

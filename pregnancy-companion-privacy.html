Privacy Policy for Pregnancy Companion

Last Updated: April 21, 2026

MeenuApp ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Pregnancy Companion mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Services.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not require users to create an account or provide personal identification information (such as name or email). Any data you enter into the app (tracking logs, notes) remains on your device.

2. Information Collected by Third Parties

  • Google Play Services: Used for distribution and platform features. Privacy Policy
  • AdMob (Google Mobile Ads): Used to display advertisements. Privacy Policy

3. Permissions

  • Internet Access: To load advertisements and app updates.
  • Notifications: To send pregnancy milestones and reminders.

4. Data Retention

All pregnancy tracking data, logs, and personal notes are stored locally on your device using Android DataStore / SharedPreferences. This data is never uploaded to our servers. To delete your data, simply uninstall the application.

5. Children’s Privacy

Pregnancy Companion is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
